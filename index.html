Leabra Tensorflow - Under Construction

A tensorflow + TPU exploration of LEABRA

A tensorflow + TPU exploration of the “Local, Error-driven and Associative, Biologically Realistic Algorithm” (LEABRA). For details see the wikipedia or emergent pages.

Implementation is drawn from this paper by Randall C. O’Reilly: Generalization in Interactive Networks: The Benefits of Inhibitory Competition and Hebbian Learning.
